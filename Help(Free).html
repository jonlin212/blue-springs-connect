Ways You Can Help Without Spending Money

Helping your community doesnt always require spending. There are an abundance of deeds that can significantly impact the community such as...

Volunteering

Often times, organizations need more volunteers. This can include shelters, food banks, and hospitals. Many of which are free and dont require any money except take time.

Spreading awareness

Communication can be one of the best ways to help the community. The reason is that if some people dont have enough funds or time to spend, maybe other acquaintences, friends, or collegues might be able to.

Donate items

Instead of money, maybe sending items such as school supplies, canned goods, and clothes can make a difference. They will always help families who are in need of supplies and anything and everything will help regardless if its not money.
